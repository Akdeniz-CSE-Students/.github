# Code of Conduct

## Our Student Community

Akdeniz-CSE-Students is a learning-focused community for **Akdeniz University Computer Engineering students** and contributors. We want this space to be welcoming, inclusive, and respectful for everyone.

We value:

- Kind and constructive communication
- Knowledge sharing and peer support
- Respect for different backgrounds, skill levels, and perspectives

## Expected Behavior

Community members are expected to:

- Be respectful and professional in issues, pull requests, comments, and discussions
- Give feedback that is constructive and focused on ideas, not people
- Be patient with beginners and help others learn
- Use inclusive and considerate language
- Respect privacy and academic integrity

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior is not acceptable:

- Harassment, discrimination, hate speech, or personal attacks
- Bullying, intimidation, or repeated hostile behavior
- Posting private information without permission
- Trolling, insulting, or deliberately disruptive communication
- Sharing inappropriate, offensive, or unrelated content

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces in this organization, including repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions, and other public interactions.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the maintainers by opening a private communication channel through available organization contact methods.

When reporting, include:

- What happened
- Where it happened
- Links/screenshots if available
- Any relevant context

All reports will be reviewed promptly and fairly.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct. They may take appropriate action, including warning, temporary restrictions, or removal from community spaces for behavior that violates this policy.
